Thanks To Tech ! The impossible is now achievable.

Jan 10, 2024

Our day begins and ends with technology. There's no way we can escape without using it. Nearly everything we see around us is a result or a kind of technology. It has turned once seemingly impossible thing to possible. We must understand how technology is crucial in our daily lives knowing it's both positive and negative impacts.

Technology has played a pivotal role in speeding up globalization. It has brought people come together and also has torn them apart. People from the corner of the world can interlink or communicate with others with the use of various social platforms. The rise of the media has facilitated people across the globe to know about each other's cultures, contents, music, and the way of living. Networking platforms allow the flow of information in a matter of seconds, showing how interconnected the world has become.

The world has now been digitalized. It has revolutionized every infrastructure. We no longer need to stand in a queue for hours for banking transactions. In rural areas, we can complete many works even in the house. It saves our time as well as our efforts. Digitalization, a boon from technology has made our life easier in many ways.

Everything has been digitalized with the advance use of technology which is good in a way but has also some negative impacts. The rise of digital media has increased security concerns. There's a high possibility of cybercrime and threats in recent age. There are some cons of technology. It has increased competition between companies which results in the increment of unemployment and more poverty. People are suffering from addiction and are mostly jobless due to the excessive use of technology.
